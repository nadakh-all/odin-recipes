Burger

Description

This classic burger recipe delivers a juicy, flavorful patty topped with fresh lettuce, tomato, cheese, and your choice of condiments. Perfect for a quick meal or a summer barbecue.

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 burger buns
  • 4 slices of cheese (optional)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • 4 slices of tomato
  • Ketchup and mustard
  • Pickles (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Season the ground beef with salt and pepper, then form it into 4 equal patties.
  3. Cook the patties for 4-5 minutes per side, or until desired doneness is achieved.
  4. Top each patty with a slice of cheese and let it melt.
  5. Place patties on buns and add lettuce, tomato, ketchup, mustard, and any additional toppings.
  6. Serve immediately.
