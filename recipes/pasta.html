Pasta

Description

This spaghetti carbonara recipe is creamy, rich, and easy to make. It features eggs, Parmesan cheese, pancetta, and black pepper for a savory Italian dish.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water according to package instructions.
  2. In a skillet, cook the pancetta until crispy.
  3. Beat the eggs with grated Parmesan and black pepper in a bowl.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and quickly mix with the egg mixture and pancetta.
  5. Serve immediately with extra Parmesan and black pepper on top.
